Cantaloupe Mint Agua Fresca

This cantaloupe agua fresca tastes like the most wonderful fresh melon water!

Ingredients

  • 1 cantaloupe (3.5 pounds, or 4 cups chopped fruit)
  • 1.5 cups cold water
  • 1 tbsp fresh lemon juice (or lime juice)
  • a few sprigs fresh mint (or basil)
  • ice

Method

  1. Cut the cantaloupe up by first cutting in half, then into quarters, and cut the flesh away from the rind. Cut into chunks and place into a blender. Blend until fully pureed, then strain the puree through a fine mesh strainer in order to collect the juice.
  2. Pour the juice into a pitcher and add the water, fresh lemon juice, and mint. Refrigerate for at least one hour so itâ€™s thoroughly chilled and the flavors are melded.
  3. Pour over ice and enjoy!
